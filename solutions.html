HUMAN RESOURCES MANAGEMENT

Axis is a module of our ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) solution which will enables you to put the right people in the right jobs, develop and reward top performers, retain key talent for the long term, and increase efficiency and operating performance throughout your departments, thus becoming an integral part of your organization. FINANCE CONTROL

Dinar is the module of our ERP solution that covers the accounting and finance control needs of the modern organization, to ensure correct budget projections, optimal financial planning and rapid response to deviations.

Its customizable notifications subsystem will allows you to alert automatically your staff on a need-to-know basis, making sure your organization stays on track.

STOCK CONTROL

eStock is an web-based inventory tracking and planning solution that allows organizations of any size to keep their assets under control by knowing the quantity, location and time for any item purchased, stored, relocated or delivered.

It advanced analytics module aids in the realization of accurate projections to maintain an optimal flow of inventory and avoid planning setbacks such as lack of or displaced materials.
